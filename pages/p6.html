E.O.T.O: Each One Teach One (Social Service)

The Significance of Community Service: -

“Remember that the happiest people are not those getting more, but those giving more.”
― H. Jackson Brown Jr.

Engaging in community service gives us a chance to become an active member of the community and leave a positive impact. It provides us a chance to acquire life skills and provide service to those who need it the most. This is beneficial for both us and the individuals as well the society. Community service can be a way for us to explore our interests and show us a possible career path. By engaging in community service, we come to discover our hidden passions and truly make a difference. This can be very beneficial for the individuals. It can help in building relationship with both peers and adults this exposes us to the real world and gives us an experience as to how the world works. It also gives an opportunity to apply the theoretical and academic knowledge accumulated in real life events. This prepares us for the future. Lastly and most importantly it helps us develop a sense of social responsibility and form a moral virtue of always “giving back” and always staying sympathetic towards those who have less than us.
